Analyzing Macro Over-Capitulation

Notice how in heavy systemic deleveraging events (2015 and 2022), Bitcoin doesn't just stop at the line—it overshoots by nearly 28% to 30% due to forced margin liquidations before establishing a hard bottom. In standard market corrections (2018, 2020, and the active 2026 phase), deviation remains tightly constrained within 0.5% to 4%.
